Courses Offered

B.A. English

A three-year undergraduate programme focusing on English literature, linguistics, communication, and language skills. Students engage with classical and contemporary texts alongside language lab sessions.

B.A. Sociology

A three-year programme exploring social structures, human behaviour, community development, and social research methods. Prepares students for social work, administration, and further research.

B.Com — Commerce

A three-year undergraduate programme covering accounting, finance, taxation, business law, and commerce. Includes Tally and GST certification as value-added components.

B.Sc. Computer Science

A three-year programme in programming, data structures, database management, networking, and software development. Equips students for IT careers and higher studies.

B.Sc. Statistics

A three-year programme covering statistical theory, probability, data analysis, and mathematical methods. Opens pathways to research, banking, and analytics sectors.
