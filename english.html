Head of the Department — Department of English

Dr. A. Subbulakshmi leads the Department of English with expertise in English literature and language studies. Under her guidance, the department has grown in academic excellence and student participation in literary activities.

She is committed to making quality English education accessible to students from all backgrounds, helping them build communication skills essential for professional and academic success.

History of the Department

The Department of English was established in the year 2020 when Government Arts and Science College, Sankarankovil was founded. Since its inception, the department has been committed to developing English language proficiency and literary awareness among students from rural and semi-urban backgrounds of the Tenkasi district.

Over the years, the department has grown in size and academic stature, conducting seminars, workshops, and guest lectures to supplement classroom learning. The department plays a key role in strengthening communication skills across all disciplines of the college.

About the Department

The Department of English offers the B.A. English programme, a three-year undergraduate course affiliated to Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli. The department is committed to developing proficiency in English language, literature, and communication among students from rural and semi-urban backgrounds.

The department emphasises both classical and contemporary English literature, linguistics, and communication skills. Students are trained through language lab sessions, literary club activities, seminars, and guest lectures by eminent scholars. The programme prepares students for careers in education, media, communication, civil services, and further postgraduate studies.

Student Activities

The Department organizes various co-curricular and extracurricular activities allowing students to explore their potential and apply theoretical knowledge in practice.

  • Literary Club: Regular debating, essay writing, and poetry recitation events.
  • Language Lab: Hands-on training on phonetics, pronunciation, and spoken English.
  • Guest Lectures: Interactive sessions by eminent scholars on literature and soft skills.
  • Seminars: Inter-departmental and national seminars encouraging student paper presentations.

Achievements

Our students and faculty continuously strive for excellence, bringing laurels to the Department and the College.

  • University Ranks: Consistent top percentiles in Manonmaniam Sundaranar University examinations.
  • Paper Presentations: Over 15 papers presented by students at National Seminars.
  • Faculty Publications: Our HOD and faculty members have published several research papers in UGC CARE listed journals.
  • Sports & Culture: Outstanding performances by English department students in cultural fests and state-level sports events.
