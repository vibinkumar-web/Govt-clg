Academic Calendar 2025–26

June 2025 — Odd Semester Begins

Commencement of classes for all UG programmes. Orientation programme for first-year students during the first week.

August 2025 — Internal Assessment I

First cycle of continuous internal assessment tests for all semesters. Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance.

October 2025 — Mid-Semester Break & Cultural Fest

Annual cultural festival “Kalai Vizha” and inter-collegiate competitions. Mid-semester break from 20–25 October.

November 2025 — Odd Semester Examinations

End-semester examinations commence. Results published within 30 days of the last examination.

December 2025 — Even Semester Begins

Even semester classes begin after the winter vacation. Bridge courses offered for academically weaker students.

February 2026 — Internal Assessment II

Second cycle of internal tests. Project work and field study submissions for final-year students.

March 2026 — Even Semester Examinations

End-semester examinations. Practical examinations and viva-voce for laboratory-based subjects.

April 2026 — Convocation & Summer Break

Annual convocation ceremony. Summer internships and value-added courses offered during the break.

Examination System

Government Arts and Science College, Sankarankovil conducts its examinations under the guidelines of Manonmaniam Sundaranar University. The evaluation system is designed to assess both theoretical knowledge and practical competence.

Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA): Each course carries 25 marks for internal assessment, comprising written tests, assignments, seminars, and attendance. Two CIA tests are conducted per semester.

End-Semester Examination (ESE): The remaining 75 marks are evaluated through a comprehensive written examination at the end of each semester. Practical courses include laboratory examinations and viva-voce.

Grading System: The college follows a 10-point CGPA system in accordance with UGC guidelines. Grade sheets and consolidated mark statements are issued to students upon completion of each semester.

Supplementary Examinations: Students who fail to clear any paper are given the opportunity to appear for supplementary examinations conducted twice a year.
