About Our College

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Established in June 2020, Government Arts and Science College, Sankarankovil has grown into one of the premier government institutions in Tamil Nadu. Affiliated to Manonmaniam Sundaranar University and reaccredited with NAAC ‘A’ Grade, our college offers a vibrant academic environment that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and holistic development.

Spread across a lush 25-acre campus in the heart of Sankarankovil, we serve over 775 students with 5 departments offering undergraduate programmes in Arts, Science, and Commerce.
