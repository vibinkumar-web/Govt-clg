Head of the Department — Department of Commerce

Dr. G. Venugopal leads the Department of Commerce with extensive expertise in commerce and business studies. He provides academic leadership and mentors students towards their professional goals in commerce and finance.

Under his guidance, students are well-prepared for placement opportunities in the banking, finance, and corporate sectors as well as for higher studies in M.Com, MBA, and professional accountancy courses like CA and CMA.

History of the Department

The Department of Commerce was established in 2020 alongside the college. From its founding, the department has focused on delivering quality commerce education to students from the Tenkasi region, preparing them for the demands of a rapidly evolving economic landscape.

Since inception, the department has organised industry visits, guest lectures by chartered accountants, and certification workshops to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

About the Department

The Department of Commerce offers the B.Com programme, a three-year undergraduate course affiliated to Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli. The department prepares students for careers in accounting, finance, banking, taxation, and business management.

The curriculum covers core commerce subjects including Financial Accounting, Business Law, Corporate Accounting, Income Tax, and Auditing. Value-added certification courses in Tally ERP and GST are integrated to enhance employability. Students are also trained in entrepreneurship and practical business skills.

Student Activities

Our students participate actively in events designed to mould them into capable business minds:

  • Commerce Club (Commercium): Regular quizzes, case study competitions, and mock stock exchange events.
  • Industrial Visits: Field trips to manufacturing units and financial hubs to understand practical nuances.
  • Workshops: Hands-on training sessions in Tally ERP 9, GST filing, and digital marketing.
  • Entrepreneurship Cell: Guidance and networking for aspiring student startup founders.

Achievements

The Department of Commerce frequently celebrates milestones set by its faculty and students:

  • Placements: Over 40% of our graduates get placed in prominent banking and financial institutions immediately after graduation.
  • Professional Exams: Several students successfully cleared the CA Foundation and CMA Inter exams over the last academic year.
  • Faculty Research: Our faculty actively contribute to reputed national journals discussing tax reforms and corporate governance.
