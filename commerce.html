About the Department

The Department of Commerce offers the B.Com programme, a three-year undergraduate course affiliated to Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli. The department prepares students for careers in accounting, finance, banking, taxation, and business management.

The curriculum covers core commerce subjects including Financial Accounting, Business Law, Corporate Accounting, Income Tax, and Auditing. Value-added certification courses in Tally ERP and GST are integrated to enhance employability. Students are also trained in entrepreneurship and practical business skills.

The department actively encourages students to appear for competitive examinations such as CA Foundation, CMA, and government banking exams. Regular seminars, industry visits, and guest lectures by chartered accountants and business professionals enrich the academic experience.

Assistant Professor & Head — Department of Commerce

The Department of Commerce is headed by a dedicated faculty member with expertise in Financial Accounting and Business Studies. The HOD provides academic leadership and mentors students towards their professional goals in commerce and finance.

Under the department's guidance, students are well-prepared for placement opportunities in the banking, finance, and corporate sectors as well as for higher studies in M.Com, MBA, and professional accountancy courses.

Syllabus — B.Com

The syllabus is prescribed by Manonmaniam Sundaranar University and is subject to revision as per UGC and university norms. The programme spans six semesters.

Semester I & II — Foundation

  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Organisation and Management
  • Tamil / Other Language (Part I)
  • English Communication Skills (Part II)
  • Environmental Studies

Semester III & IV — Core Studies

  • Corporate Accounting
  • Business Law and Company Law
  • Cost Accounting
  • Banking Theory, Law and Practice
  • Allied: Business Mathematics / Statistics for Commerce

Semester V & VI — Advanced & Electives

  • Income Tax Law and Practice
  • Auditing and Assurance
  • Management Accounting
  • GST and Indirect Taxation
  • Elective: Tally ERP / E-Commerce / Entrepreneurship Development
  • Project Work & Viva Voce

For the complete and updated syllabus, refer to the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University official syllabus document or contact the department office.
