History of the College

Government Arts and Science College, Sankarankovil, was founded in 2020 to light the lamp of higher education in the economically and socially backward region of Sankarankovil, Tenkasi District, Tamil Nadu. The primary aim of the institution is to provide quality education to learners from the downtrodden sections of society living in and around Sankarankovil. The majority of the students are first-generation learners.

The college was inaugurated on 17-06-2020 at private temporary premises in Seevalarayanenthal village, Sankarankovil taluk. Later on, the college was moved on to the present permanent Government building which was inaugurated by the Honourable Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu on 22nd January 2024 through video-conferencing mode.

Government Arts and Science College is located on Tiruvengadam Road, a campus covering 3.31 acres of land allotted to the Department of Higher Education [G.O.No.52, J1 Department, dated 13-03-2020]. It stands self-contained and prominent with all amenities.

The institution is affiliated with Manomaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli. It is a co-educational institution offering five UG Programmes (B.A. English, B.A. Sociology, B.Com Commerce, B.Sc. Computer Science, B.Sc. Statistics). Within a short span of five years, the College has registered its name for its committed service towards the round and holistic development of the students individually and academically. Co-curricular and extracurricular activities including sports are encouraged to ensure multi-dimensional personality development of the learners.

Currently, 775 students are pursuing their studies at this college. Additionally, 29 teaching staff and 11 non-teaching staff are working here.

Vision & Mission

🎯 Our Vision

  • To Provide Quality Higher Education to all Sections of the Society.
  • To Inculcate Knowledge and skills and empower students to be self-reliant individuals.

🎯 Our Mission

  • To provide a quality education in basic Arts, Science, and Humanities.
  • To Provide inexpensive educational services to the downtrodden and weaker sections of the society.
  • To establish a Centre for imparting knowledge, enhancing skills, and cultivating attitudes among the students.
  • To Develop responsible leaders for the future.
  • To Develop courage, confidence, and competitiveness in the changing global scenario.
  • To Motivate the faculty members in academic, research, and extension activities.

🎯 Motto

Education is the whole.

🎯 Broad Objectives

  • To help students from economically weaker sections of society access quality higher education.
  • To inculcate punctuality, discipline, and regularity.
  • To equip the students to face the challenges of modern society from professional, cultural, and moral perspectives.
  • To Enhance the life skills by providing value-based education.
  • To create a conducive environment for students to achieve academic excellence to face global challenges.

Governing Body

The college is guided by an experienced governing body comprising eminent academicians, industrialists, and community leaders who ensure that the institution stays true to its founding ideals.
