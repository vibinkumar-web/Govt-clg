Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your admission at Government Arts and Science College, Sankarankovil for the academic year 2026–27.

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for your chosen programme. Ensure you meet the minimum academic requirements before applying.

Download Application

Obtain the application form from the college website or the Admissions Office. Online application is also available.

Submit Documents

Attach photocopies of mark sheets, transfer certificate, community certificate, and passport-size photographs with the filled application.

Merit Selection

Selection is based on academic merit. A provisional merit list will be published on the college notice board and website.

Counselling

Shortlisted candidates must attend the counselling session with original documents for verification and seat allotment.

Pay Fees & Enrol

Complete the fee payment at the college office. Collect your ID card and class schedule to begin your academic journey.

Eligibility Criteria

Programme Qualification Minimum Marks Duration
B.A. (Arts) Higher Secondary (12th) — Any Stream 50% Aggregate 3 Years
B.Sc. (Science) Higher Secondary (12th) — Science Stream 55% in Science Subjects 3 Years
B.Com (Commerce) Higher Secondary (12th) — Commerce / Any Stream 50% Aggregate 3 Years
BCA Higher Secondary (12th) with Mathematics 50% in Maths 3 Years

* Relaxation of 5% marks for SC/ST/OBC candidates as per government norms. Age limit as per university regulations.
